  • Career and development

Who will you meet

This Summit shall bring together a total of 1000 youths drawn from all the local Government Councils in Kebbi state and a few from other from other part of Nigeria and beyond. It is prudent for emphasis to be placed on the Nigeria Youth as they are the pillars of transformation and progress in our societies. A few of the participants shall comprise of professionals, leading politicians and opinion shapers, journalists and other relevant stakeholders especially those dealing with youth and other marginalized persons in various areas as the Summit Organizing Committee shall deem necessary.

Expected outcome

The Summit Secretariat envisages achieving the following; Ø An upsurge in youth participation in sustainable development and other societal issues in Kebbi State and in other Northern States . Ø A better understanding of the concepts of sustainability and the roles of the youths in the future society. Ø A clearly defined roadmap for sustainable development in Kebbi State Ø Strengthened network of youths and organizations dealing in programme and activities aimed at liberating the youths from the shackles of poverty, unemployment and drug abuse. Ø Re-energized youth ready to take head-on the challenges of our society
